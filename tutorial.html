Hi there! Welcome to the Hangman Tutorial.

At the start of each game, you will be assigned a random category and word.

The difficulty buttons determine how many wrong guesses you can make before the game is over.

If you get stuck, you can click the hint button at the bottom left corner of the game screen.

Use it wisely, as using a hint uses up one of your guess attempts!

Good luck, and have fun playing Hangman!
